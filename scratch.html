Who I Am

I'm Tanner Davis — a BYU Pre-Business student, entrepreneur, returned missionary, and lifelong outdoorsman from Highland, Utah. I believe in working hard, serving others, and never missing a chance to get outside. Whether I'm analyzing financial data, leading a team of volunteers, or catching waves, I bring the same energy and commitment to everything I do.

I'm passionate about finance, leadership, and building meaningful relationships. My goal is to combine my entrepreneurial experience with a strong business education to make a real impact in the world of finance.

My Journey

  1. Growing Up in Highland, UT
    • Earned my Eagle Scout award through the Boy Scouts of America
    • Developed a love for the outdoors — fishing, hiking, and camping became my escapes
    • Started Tanner's Lawn Care at age 12 and grew it for 7 years
  2. Lone Peak High School (Graduated 2023)
    • Maintained a 4.0 GPA and earned AP Scholar recognition
    • Led the Unified Special Olympics club as Student Head
    • Received the Regents Scholarship and graduated with honors
  3. Volunteer Missionary in Bakersfield, CA (2023–2025)
    • Served a two-year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints
    • Achieved fluency in Spanish through full cultural immersion
    • Led groups of 16+ missionaries and presented 50+ workshops
  4. Brigham Young University (2025–Present)
    • Pursuing a Pre-Business track with plans to study finance
    • Building on my entrepreneurial and analytical background
    • Excited to enter the professional finance world
